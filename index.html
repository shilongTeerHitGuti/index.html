About Shillong Teer

Shillong Teer is a traditional archery-based lottery game played in the state of Meghalaya, India. The game is organized by the Khasi Hills Archery Sports Association, and it has become a part of local culture and entertainment. Every day, except Sundays and holidays, two rounds of archery are held — known as the First Round (FR) and Second Round (SR). The results are based on the number of arrows hitting the target, and players bet on the last two digits of the total hits.
